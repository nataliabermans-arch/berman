### What Is Platelet-Rich Plasma (PRP) Therapy?

So, what exactly is this “liquid gold” that powers the O-Shot? Platelet-Rich Plasma (PRP) therapy starts with a simple blood draw, just like you’d have at a routine doctor’s visit. That blood sample is then placed in a centrifuge, a machine that spins it at high speed to separate its components. This process isolates the platelets and plasma, creating a concentrated serum packed with powerful growth factors. These growth factors are your body’s natural repair crew, responsible for healing wounds and regenerating tissue. Once the PRP is prepared, it is carefully injected into specific, numbed areas of the vagina, primarily around the clitoris and the G-spot.
