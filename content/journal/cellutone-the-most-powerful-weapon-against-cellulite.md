Did you know that cellulite is completely normal?An estimated 80%-98% of the US population have some appearance of cellulite. It is characterized as a localized skin condition that causes a lumpy, dimpled appearance on the surface of the skin. Cellulite is most common on the thighs, hips, buttocks, and abdomen and is most prevalent in women. However, contrary to popular belief, cellulite is not an indication of being out of shape either!

Many women that exercise frequently have cellulite that they have difficulty getting rid of. Although cellulite may be normal, many women find themselves still wanting a solution to rid themselves of the unsightly lumps and bumps.

There are a plethora of creams and topical lotions that promise to treat cellulite, but many women have found these to be unreliable.There are also more drastic options, which include surgery.
