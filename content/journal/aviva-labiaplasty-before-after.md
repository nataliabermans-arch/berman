## How Does the Aviva Treatment Work?

If you’re looking for a way to change the appearance of your labia without the downtime and scarring of traditional surgery, you’ve likely come across Aviva. So, what exactly is it? Think of [Aviva](/aviva/) as a modern, less invasive approach to labiaplasty. The treatment works by delivering radiofrequency (RF) energy beneath the skin’s surface. A very thin probe is inserted through a tiny entry point—no large incisions needed. This RF energy gently heats the underlying tissue, causing it to contract and shrink. At the same time, this process stimulates your body’s natural collagen production, which helps firm and tighten the skin over time. The result is a refined contour and a reduction in excess tissue, all achieved through a minimally invasive technique that prioritizes your comfort and a quick recovery.

### How Radiofrequency Sculpts and Tightens

The magic behind Aviva really comes down to radiofrequency energy. This technology has been used in medical and aesthetic treatments for years because it’s incredibly effective at remodeling tissue from the inside out. Second, and perhaps more importantly, it signals your body to kickstart the production of new, healthy collagen. This new collagen helps to firm, smooth, and reshape the labial tissue for a natural-looking and long-lasting result. ## Frequently Asked Questions

**Is the [Aviva](/aviva/) procedure painful?** This is easily one of the most common questions, and the short answer is no. The treatment area is completely numbed with a local anesthetic before the procedure begins, so you won’t feel any pain—just some light pressure or a gentle warming sensation. Afterward, you can expect some mild swelling and tenderness for a few days, but most women find it’s easily managed with over-the-counter pain relievers and is far less intense than recovering from traditional surgery.

**How soon will I see my final results from Aviva?** You’ll likely notice a difference almost immediately due to the contraction of the tissue. However, the most significant results appear gradually. The radiofrequency energy stimulates your body to produce new collagen, a process that takes a few months. You can expect to see your final, settled outcome about three to six months after your treatment, once that new collagen has fully developed and remodeled the area.

**Are the results from Aviva permanent?** Yes, the results are considered permanent because the procedure physically remodels and tightens the labial tissue. The changes you see are long-lasting. Of course, future life events like pregnancy or significant hormonal shifts can create new changes in the tissue, but the improvements from the Aviva treatment itself do not fade away over time.

**Can Aviva be combined with other vaginal rejuvenation treatments?** Absolutely. Aviva is specifically designed to address the external labia. Many women choose to pair it with other treatments that target different concerns, such as internal vaginal laxity, urinary incontinence, or dryness. Creating a combination treatment plan is a great way to achieve comprehensive results, and it’s something you can discuss during your personal consultation to see what makes the most sense for your goals.

**What’s the main difference between Aviva and a traditional labiaplasty?** The simplest way to think about it is that Aviva achieves a similar aesthetic goal without the surgery. A traditional labiaplasty uses a scalpel to cut away tissue, which requires stitches and leaves scars. Aviva uses controlled heat to remodel tissue from within, so there are no incisions and no scars. This also means the recovery is much faster—we’re talking a couple of days of downtime instead of several weeks.
