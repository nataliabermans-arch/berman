## What Exactly is Bio-Identical HRT?

To put it simply, **[Bio-Identical Hormone Replacement Therapy](/testosterone-hormone-replacement-therapy/)** (BHRT) uses hormones that are chemically identical to the ones your body naturally produces. These hormones come from plant sources like yams or soy and are customized to fit your individual hormonal needs. Because they mimic your body’s natural hormones, BHRT is often better tolerated and can provide relief from symptoms linked to menopause or other hormonal issues.

## What Makes Bio-Identical Hormones Different?

You might be wondering how bio-identical testosterone replacement therapy differs from traditional hormone replacement therapies. The main distinction is in their structure. Bio-identical hormones are identical on a molecular level to the ones your body naturally produces. On the other hand, traditional hormones are often made from synthetic materials or even animal hormones, which your body may not process as well.

For many women, this makes bio-identical hormones a gentler and more natural option. Because these hormones are a perfect match for your body, they can provide symptom relief without some of the side effects that can come with synthetic hormones. Plus, since the treatment is highly personalized, you’re not getting a blanket dose. Instead, it’s crafted to meet your specific hormonal needs.
