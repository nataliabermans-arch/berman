You’ll feel a combination of a warming sensation, which is the radiofrequency at work, along with strong muscle contractions. It feels like you’re doing an incredibly intense workout while lying down and relaxing. The intensity is gradually increased throughout the session to a level that’s challenging but comfortable for you. **How is Emsculpt Neo different from other body sculpting treatments like CoolSculpting?** The biggest difference is that Emsculpt Neo is the only treatment that builds muscle while it reduces fat. Many other popular body contouring procedures, like CoolSculpting, focus exclusively on eliminating fat cells. Emsculpt Neo uses two technologies at once to give you a more comprehensive result: radiofrequency to permanently reduce fat and HIFEM+ energy to build and tone muscle, creating a stronger and more sculpted appearance. **Are the results from Emsculpt Neo permanent?** The results are long-lasting, but it’s a two-part answer. The fat reduction is considered permanent because the treatment destroys fat cells, and your body clears them away for good. The muscle you build is real and can last for a long time, but just like the muscle you build at the gym, it requires maintenance. A healthy diet, regular exercise, and occasional follow-up sessions will help you keep your toned results for the long term. **Can I use this treatment to replace my gym routine?** Think of Emsculpt Neo as a powerful partner to your healthy lifestyle, not a replacement for it. It’s designed to help you achieve a level of muscle definition and fat reduction that can be difficult to get from exercise alone. It’s perfect for targeting stubborn areas and enhancing the hard work you’re already putting in. The best and most lasting results come from combining the treatment with consistent activity and a balanced diet. **What should I expect to feel after a session is over?** There is absolutely no downtime required after an Emsculpt Neo session, so you can return to your normal activities immediately. In the day or two following your treatment, you might feel some muscle soreness in the targeted area, similar to how you’d feel after a challenging workout. This is a positive sign that the HIFEM+ technology has effectively stimulated your muscles.
