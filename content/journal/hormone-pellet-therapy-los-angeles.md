If you’re feeling like you’re not quite yourself lately, dealing with things like fatigue, mood swings, or a lagging libido, your hormones might be the reason. ### How Do Hormone Pellets Work?

The process is refreshingly simple. During a quick in-office visit, the tiny hormone pellet is inserted under the skin, usually in the upper hip area. Once it’s in place, the pellet gets to work, slowly and consistently releasing hormones directly into your bloodstream. This steady delivery system is a game-changer because it mimics your body’s natural hormone production, avoiding the frustrating highs and lows that can come with other methods like pills or creams. The pellets dissolve on their own over several months, so there’s no need for removal. It’s a hassle-free way to maintain hormonal balance and keep your symptoms at bay.

### What Are Bioidentical Hormones?

The term “bioidentical” simply means the hormones in the pellets are molecularly identical to the ones your body produces naturally. These hormones, like estradiol and testosterone, are derived from plant sources and are designed to be a perfect match for your body’s chemistry. Because your body recognizes them as its own, it can use them effectively. This is a key reason why many women find [Bioidentical Hormone Replacement Therapy](/biote-hormone-therapy/) to be a gentle yet powerful solution. ## What to Expect During the Procedure

The idea of starting a new treatment can feel a little intimidating, but the process for hormone pellet therapy is designed to be simple and comfortable. It’s a straightforward journey that begins with understanding your unique needs and ends with a quick, in-office procedure with minimal downtime. ### Your Initial Hormone Assessment

Everything starts with a conversation. Before any treatment begins, you’ll have a thorough consultation to discuss your symptoms, health history, and wellness goals. This initial meeting is crucial because it allows us to get a complete picture of what’s going on. We’ll also run comprehensive lab tests to get a precise reading of your current hormone levels. ### The Quick In-Office Insertion

Once your custom hormone pellet is ready, you’ll come in for the insertion. This part of the process is surprisingly fast and simple, usually taking less than 15 minutes. We’ll start by numbing a small area on your upper hip or buttock so you won’t feel any discomfort. Then, a tiny incision is made, and the pellet, which is often smaller than a grain of rice, is placed just under the skin. The incision is then closed with a small piece of medical tape or a stitch. The pellet itself is designed to dissolve and be absorbed by your body over several months, so there’s no need for a follow-up removal procedure.

### Recovery and Aftercare

One of the best parts of pellet therapy is that there’s virtually no recovery time. You can get back to your day right after your appointment. We’ll place a small bandage over the insertion site, and you’ll just need to keep it clean and dry for a few days. To ensure the pellet stays in place and the area heals properly, we recommend avoiding strenuous exercise, heavy lifting, hot tubs, and swimming for about three to seven days. Other than those minor adjustments, you can continue with your normal routine. We’ll provide you with a full set of simple aftercare instructions to make the healing process as smooth as possible.
