## Meet Dr. Jennifer Berman

When you’re looking for a doctor to discuss sensitive topics like sexual health or menopause, you want someone who is not only an expert but also someone you can trust. Dr. Jennifer Berman is a leading figure in women’s health, known for her compassionate approach and pioneering work. She has dedicated her career to creating a space where women feel heard, understood, and empowered to take control of their well-being. From her rigorous academic training to her familiar face on national television, Dr. Berman has consistently worked to bring women’s health issues into the mainstream conversation, making complex topics accessible and manageable for everyone.

### Her Educational Journey

Dr. Berman’s path to becoming a renowned specialist is built on a solid foundation of academic excellence and highly specialized training. Her commitment to medicine began early and was honed through years of dedicated study at some of the country’s top institutions. This rigorous background prepared her to tackle complex issues in female urology and sexual health, fields that require a deep understanding of both the physical and emotional aspects of women’s wellness. Her educational journey reflects a lifelong passion for learning and a drive to provide the best possible care for her patients.

#### Academic Foundations

Dr. Berman’s academic career began at Hollins College (now Hollins University), where she graduated in 1986. She continued her education at the University of Maryland Medical Center, earning a Master of Science degree in 1989. This early training provided her with the scientific and research skills that would become the bedrock of her medical practice, instilling a detail-oriented and evidence-based approach to patient care that she is known for today.

#### Medical Degree and Specialized Training

After building her foundational knowledge, Dr. Berman earned her medical degree from the prestigious Boston University School of Medicine. She then completed her urology residency at the University of Maryland, followed by a highly specialized fellowship at the UCLA Medical Center. There, she focused specifically on female urology and pelvic floor reconstructive surgery. This advanced training put her at the forefront of a new and evolving field, equipping her with the unique expertise to address health concerns that many women face. You can learn more about her impressive background on her [about page](/about/).

### A Career Dedicated to Women’s Health

Dr. Berman didn’t just enter the field of women’s health; she helped shape it. Throughout her career, she has been a trailblazer, creating programs and spaces dedicated to addressing the specific needs of women. Her work has been instrumental in establishing female sexual medicine as a legitimate and crucial area of healthcare. By founding her own wellness center, she created a sanctuary where women can receive personalized, cutting-edge treatments in a supportive and understanding environment, truly dedicating her life’s work to improving women’s quality of life.

#### Pioneering Female Sexual Medicine at UCLA

In 2001, Dr. Berman made history when she became the first-ever director of the Female Sexual Medicine Center at UCLA. This was a landmark achievement, as it was one of the first university-affiliated programs of its kind. In this role, she established a new standard of care, combining clinical practice with research to better understand and treat female sexual dysfunction. Her work at UCLA was pivotal in bringing these often-ignored health issues into the light of academic medicine.

#### Founding The Berman Women’s Wellness Center

Driven by a vision to provide comprehensive and integrated care, Dr. Berman later opened her private practice in Beverly Hills, founding The Berman Women’s Wellness Center. This center is the culmination of her life’s work, offering a holistic approach to women’s health that addresses everything from menopause and [low libido](/low-libido-and-your-relationships/) to urinary incontinence. It’s a place where she can offer innovative treatments and personalized care plans, ensuring every patient feels empowered on their journey to optimal health and wellness.

### Bringing Women’s Health to the Forefront

Beyond her clinical practice, Dr. Berman has been a powerful advocate for women’s health in the public sphere. She recognized early on that open, honest conversations are key to breaking down stigmas and empowering women with knowledge. Through her work in media, including television appearances and writing, she has translated complex medical information into practical advice that has reached millions. Her ability to connect with a broad audience has made her one of the most trusted voices in her field.

#### From the TV Screen to Your Bookshelf

Dr. Berman extended her reach as an educator by co-authoring the book, “For Women Only: A Revolutionary Guide to Overcoming Sexual Dysfunction and Reclaiming Your Sex Life.” This book provided women everywhere with a valuable resource, offering clear guidance and hope for issues that were once considered taboo. By putting this information into an accessible format, she helped normalize the conversation around female sexual health and provided actionable steps for women to improve their lives.

#### Notable Media Appearances

You may recognize Dr. Berman from her numerous appearances on national television. She has shared her expertise on highly respected programs like _Good Morning America_, _The Oprah Winfrey Show_, and _The Today Show_. Her ability to discuss sensitive topics with clarity and compassion has also made her a go-to expert for networks like CNN, CNBC, and HLN. These appearances have solidified her reputation as a leading authority, bringing credible medical information to a massive audience.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**What makes Dr. Berman’s approach to women’s health different from my regular gynecologist?** While your gynecologist is essential for routine care, Dr. Berman offers a more specialized focus. As a urologist who completed a fellowship in female urology and pelvic floor surgery at UCLA, she has advanced training in the complex systems that govern sexual, urinary, and hormonal health. This allows her to connect issues that might seem unrelated and provide targeted, cutting-edge treatments that go beyond standard care.

**What specific conditions does Dr. Berman treat?** Dr. Berman focuses on improving a woman’s quality of life by addressing a range of concerns. This includes managing symptoms of menopause, treating low libido, resolving urinary incontinence, and addressing issues like vaginal dryness or pelvic pain. She provides innovative, often non-invasive, solutions tailored to your specific needs and health goals.

**I’ve seen Dr. Berman on TV. Does she still actively see patients?** Yes, absolutely. While she is passionate about educating women through media, her primary focus is her private practice, The Berman Women’s Wellness Center in Beverly Hills. She is deeply committed to providing one-on-one, personalized care and helping her patients directly.

**My health concerns feel very personal and sensitive. What can I expect during a visit?** Feeling hesitant to discuss these topics is completely normal, and creating a comfortable space is Dr. Berman’s priority. You can expect a conversation, not just an examination. The goal of your visit is for her to listen and understand your experiences without judgment, so she can work with you to create a treatment plan that makes you feel confident and in control.

**How do I know if I’m a good candidate for her services?** If you’re experiencing any issue related to your sexual, urinary, or hormonal health that impacts your daily life, you are likely in the right place. Whether it’s a small leak when you laugh, a noticeable drop in your sex drive, or discomfort during intimacy, these are valid health concerns.
