## How Does [V-Tone](/v-tone/) and How Does It Work?

### What’s the Science Behind V-Tone?

So, how does V-Tone actually work? The technology behind it is called Electrical Muscle Stimulation, or EMS. During a session, V-Tone uses gentle electromagnetic energy to stimulate your [pelvic floor muscles](/sexual-urinary-tract-health/), causing them to contract thousands of times. This process is like doing a series of perfect Kegels, but far more powerful and effective than you could ever achieve on your own.

These deep, involuntary contractions rehabilitate your weakened muscles, making them stronger and more supportive. It’s a passive process for you—you just relax while the device does the work. This targeted “workout” helps re-educate your muscles, restoring their strength and function over a series of short, comfortable sessions.
