### What Causes Cellulite?

Before we dive into treatments, let’s understand the culprit. Cellulite happens when fat cells push up against the skin while the fibrous bands pull down, creating that signature dimpled look. Hormones, genetics, and even lifestyle factors can contribute to its appearance. But don’t stress — you’re definitely not alone, and we’ve got plenty of ways to tackle it.

### Treatment Options That Work

Now that we know what causes cellulite, it’s time to talk solutions. At The Berman Women’s Wellness Center, Dr Berman tailors cutting-edge treatments that target cellulite at its core. Here’s a look at what we’ve got in store:

If you’re looking for a game-changer, Morpheus 8 might just be it. This treatment uses radiofrequency energy combined with microneedling to target deeper layers of the skin. Boosting collagen production tightens and smooths the skin over time, making it firmer and more toned. #### 2\. Emtone

When you want to take cellulite head-on, Emtone is the way to go. This treatment combines radiofrequency and targeted pressure energy to reduce the appearance of cellulite while improving skin texture. It’s non-invasive, meaning no downtime, and can be used on any body part where cellulite pops up. Plus, it feels a bit like a hot stone massage — what’s not to love about that?

#### 3\. Beautifill

Want to say goodbye to unwanted fat and cellulite in one go? Beautifill could be your answer. This fat-transfer technology removes fat from areas like the abdomen or thighs and repurposes it to add volume where needed. The result? Smoother, firmer skin with the added bonus of body contouring. It’s a win-win for those looking to slim down and beat cellulite in one shot.

#### 4\. Evolve

Evolve offers a trifecta of treatments that address skin tightening, cellulite reduction, and muscle toning — all in one. Whether it’s stubborn cellulite on the thighs or loose skin around the belly, Evolve has you covered. By targeting problem areas with radiofrequency energy, it improves both skin texture and muscle tone, giving you that smooth, sculpted look you’ve been dreaming of.
