I am proud to announce that I am partnering with Plan B One-Step to help educate women about [sexual & reproductive health.](/)

Having dedicated my career to advancing women’s sexual health, I’m incredibly excited to announce my partnership with Plan B One-Step! When your ‘Plan A’ for birth control doesn’t go as planned, here’s what you need to know: Plan B is a backup option that helps to prevent pregnancy before it starts. It’s available right off the shelf at all major retailers and you don’t need a prescription or ID to purchase. Stay tuned for more from me on this topic— I’ll be working with Plan B to continue educating women about their reproductive health issues and concerns.
