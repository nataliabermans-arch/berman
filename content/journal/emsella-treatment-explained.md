#### Contraindications: Who Should Avoid Emsella?

While Emsella is a safe and effective treatment for many women, your health and safety are the top priorities. For this reason, it’s important to know that it isn’t the right fit for everyone. You should avoid Emsella treatment if you have any of the following:

- **Pregnancy:** Emsella is not recommended if you are pregnant. The effects of the electromagnetic energy on a developing fetus have not been studied, so it’s essential to wait until after you’ve given birth.

- **Certain Medical Conditions:** It’s crucial to discuss your full health history with your provider. Conditions like malignant tumors, hemorrhagic conditions, or severe heart disorders may make you ineligible for treatment.
