This week’s “WORK” panel featuring Dr. Berman covers social media topics, including becoming an influencer. Dr. Berman joins the panel with Heather Dubrow along with several other experts to share their perspectives on this trend. Many influencers are connecting with their audiences through an emerging list of platforms, with TikTok and Snapchat the newest ones among them. In addition, the growth of social media influencers has become a bigger factor in many business sectors.
