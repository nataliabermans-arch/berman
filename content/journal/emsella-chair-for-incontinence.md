The Emsella chair uses advanced electromagnetic energy to create thousands of deep, comprehensive contractions across the entire pelvic floor in a single session, strengthening the muscles far more effectively than you could on your own.

**What does the treatment actually feel like?** It’s a unique sensation, but it isn’t painful. You’ll feel a tingling in your pelvic region along with distinct muscle contractions. **Will I need more treatments in the future to maintain the results?** The results from your initial series of treatments are long-lasting because you are genuinely rebuilding muscle strength. **Is this treatment only for women who have had children?** Not at all. While pregnancy and childbirth are common reasons for pelvic floor weakness, they are far from the only ones. Many women seek Emsella to address incontinence caused by menopause, hormonal changes, or the natural aging process. It’s an effective solution for any woman looking to strengthen her pelvic floor and regain control, regardless of whether she has given birth.
