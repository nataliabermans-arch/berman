## **Surgical Methods of Vaginal Rejuvenation**

### Labiaplasty Techniques

One of the most well-known surgical options is labiaplasty. This procedure is designed to change the size or shape of the labia—either the inner lips (labia minora) or the outer lips (labia majora). Women choose to have a labiaplasty for a variety of personal reasons. For some, it’s about physical comfort; elongated labia can sometimes cause chafing, twisting, or discomfort during exercise, intimacy, or when wearing certain types of clothing. For others, the motivation is aesthetic, aiming for a look that makes them feel more confident. The procedure is tailored to each individual’s anatomy and desired outcome, focusing on creating a result that feels natural and comfortable for them.

#### Laser Reduction and Reshaping

Modern surgical techniques often incorporate advanced tools, and lasers are a great example. In the context of vaginal rejuvenation, laser procedures can be used for precise tissue reshaping and tightening. Laser Vaginal Rejuvenation, for instance, is a specific surgical method that aims to tighten the vaginal canal, which can enhance sexual sensation and provide better support for the bladder, sometimes helping with mild stress urinary incontinence. The laser’s energy promotes collagen remodeling, leading to firmer tissue. While this is a surgical approach, similar results for tissue tightening can often be achieved with non-surgical, energy-based treatments like [Votiva](/vaginal-rejuvenation-expert/), which uses radiofrequency to achieve its effects with minimal downtime.

### Perineoplasty

The perineum is the area of skin and muscle between the vaginal opening and the anus, and it can be significantly affected by childbirth. A perineoplasty is a surgical procedure designed to repair and reconstruct this area. It’s often sought by women who have experienced tearing during delivery or feel that the vaginal opening has become too wide or lax. By repairing the underlying muscles and removing excess skin, a perineoplasty can restore the structure and tightness of the vaginal entrance. This can not only improve the cosmetic appearance of the area but also enhance friction and sensation during intercourse for both partners.

### Other Procedures for the Outer Vaginal Area

Beyond the labia and the vaginal canal, several other procedures focus on refining the appearance and contour of the entire external genital region, known as the vulva.

#### Liposculpturing of the Mons Pubis

The mons pubis is the fleshy mound located over the pubic bone. For some women, this area can have excess fatty tissue that creates a bulge, which might be more noticeable in tight clothing like leggings or swimsuits. Liposculpturing of the mons pubis is a procedure that specifically targets and removes this excess fat. It’s a form of liposuction that contours the area to create a flatter, smoother profile. The goal is to create a shape that is more in proportion with the rest of the lower abdomen and vulva, helping women feel more comfortable and confident in their bodies.

#### Suprapubic Lift

A suprapubic lift, also known as a monsplasty, addresses sagging skin and tissue in the pubic area. This sagging can happen as a result of aging, significant weight loss, or pregnancy. Unlike liposculpturing, which only removes fat, a suprapubic lift involves removing excess skin and tightening the underlying tissues to lift the entire mons pubis. This creates a firmer, more toned, and more youthful appearance for the whole area. It’s a more comprehensive procedure that provides a solution when skin laxity, not just excess fat, is the primary concern for a patient.

#### Labia Majora Augmentation

While many procedures focus on reduction, some women are concerned with a loss of volume in the outer labia (labia majora). Aging and hormonal changes can cause this area to appear deflated or wrinkled. Labia majora augmentation is a procedure designed to restore a fuller, plumper appearance. This is typically done through fat transfer, where a small amount of fat is taken from another area of the body, like the thighs or abdomen, purified, and then carefully injected into the labia majora. This adds volume and smooths the skin, creating a more youthful and rejuvenated look.
