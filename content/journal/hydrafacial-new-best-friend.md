HydraFacial is a skin-refreshing treatment for patients who want a cleaner, smoother, more hydrated look without surgery or significant downtime. The treatment combines cleansing, exfoliation, gentle extraction, and infusion of hydrating serums in a single appointment, making it a practical option before an event or as part of an ongoing skin maintenance plan.

At Dr. Jennifer Berman's Beverly Hills practice, aesthetic treatments are approached as part of whole-person wellness. Skin texture, dryness, dullness, and visible congestion can all affect how confident a patient feels, especially during hormonal transitions that may change skin tone, moisture, and sensitivity. HydraFacial can help support a brighter surface appearance while staying gentle enough for many skin types.

## Why Patients Choose HydraFacial

Patients often choose HydraFacial because it is designed to be comfortable and efficient. The treatment helps remove buildup from the skin surface, clears congested pores, and replenishes hydration so the skin can look refreshed. Because there is little to no recovery time for many patients, it can fit into a busy schedule more easily than more intensive resurfacing procedures.

HydraFacial may be considered for:

- Dull or dehydrated-looking skin
- Rough texture or uneven tone
- Congested pores
- A pre-event glow
- Maintenance between other aesthetic treatments

## Is HydraFacial Right for You?

The best treatment plan depends on your skin, goals, timing, and any medical factors that may affect sensitivity or healing. A consultation allows Dr. Berman's team to evaluate whether HydraFacial should be used on its own or combined with other aesthetic and regenerative options.
