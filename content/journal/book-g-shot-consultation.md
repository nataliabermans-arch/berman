## Frequently Asked Questions

**Is the [G-Shot](/sexual-health/) procedure painful?** This is a very common question, and the short answer is no, it’s designed to be as comfortable as possible. Before the injection, a local anesthetic is applied to numb the area completely. Most women report feeling a slight pressure during the injection itself, but not pain. The entire process is incredibly quick, so any sensation is over in just a few seconds.

**How soon will I notice the effects of the G-Shot?** While you can typically resume sexual activity just a few hours after the procedure, the full effects might take a few days to become apparent. This brief period allows the filler to settle into the tissue. Many women start to notice a heightened sensitivity and a more pronounced response within the first week following their treatment.

**What happens when the G-Shot wears off?** The filler used in the G-Shot, which is typically hyaluronic acid, is naturally and safely absorbed by your body over time. As this happens, the G-spot will simply return to its previous state. There are no lasting changes or negative effects. If you love your results, you can choose to have a follow-up treatment to maintain them.

**Is the G-Shot the same thing as the O-Shot?** They are two different treatments with similar goals of enhancing sexual pleasure. The G-Shot uses a filler to physically increase the size and prominence of the G-spot, making it easier to stimulate. The O-Shot, on the other hand, uses platelet-rich plasma (PRP) from your own blood to promote tissue regeneration and improve nerve sensitivity throughout the clitoral and vaginal areas.

**Will my partner be able to feel a difference?** The primary goal of the G-Shot is to increase your personal sensation and arousal. While some partners may notice a subtle textural difference or increased fullness in the area, the most significant change they will likely experience is your heightened response and confidence. The real benefit is shared through a more connected and satisfying intimate experience for both of you.
