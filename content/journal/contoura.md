## How Contoura works

Radiofrequency energy increases the temperature under the skin, which is what kills the adipose tissue. The phenomenon is called adipocyte apoptosis or the death of cells. This helps maintain homeostasis, or the ability of the body to balance its functions.

Skin tightening happens because radiofrequency causes the contraction of tissues. The treatment for fat reduction also prevents loss of collagen on treated areas. Learn more about how the treatment works below.

### BODYFX

BodyFX is an applicator that combines radiofrequency with vacuum energy to ensure that the treated area evenly improves. The vacuum energy causes suction, which helps RF energy reach the subdermal layers of skin. This effectively heats adipose tissue, making it easier to remove them from the body. Uniform heating of the skin’s layers can help deliver optimum results for fat reduction and skin tightening.

### MINIFX

MiniFX has the same functions as the BodyFX applicator. However, with its smaller and more compact design, it is more suitable for treatment of small areas such as the face, neck, knees, and even cellulite.
