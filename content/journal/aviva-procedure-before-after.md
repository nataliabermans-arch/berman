The science behind Aviva is both simple and brilliant. The procedure uses a small, precise device to deliver controlled radiofrequency heat to the targeted tissues. Over the following weeks and months, this new collagen growth continues to remodel and firm the area from within. The entire process is performed without any large incisions or stitches, which means you get the aesthetic and functional results you want without the visible scars or lengthy recovery associated with traditional surgery.
