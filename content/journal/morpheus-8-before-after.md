At its core, Morpheus 8 is a brilliant combination of microneedling and radiofrequency (RF) energy. But Morpheus 8 takes it a step further. As the needles penetrate the skin, they also deliver gentle radiofrequency heat deep into the underlying layers. This targeted energy heats the tissue, causing existing collagen fibers to contract and tighten while further stimulating the production of new, healthy collagen. This dual-action approach allows for deeper, more dramatic remodeling of the skin than either treatment could achieve on its own.
